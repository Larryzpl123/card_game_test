Rules
A deck contains 50 cards.
The deck includes:
1 hero card, 0-5 special cards, 0-10 decision cards, 0-10 equipment cards, 1-50 action cards.
The total must be 50 cards.
At the start, remove the hero card from the deck.
Same name rules:
[Special] cards can only be carried as one.
Non-[Special] decision and equipment cards can be carried up to two.
Action cards can be carried in any number.
Initially draw 5 cards, then draw two cards per turn.
The first player cannot use decision cards or attack on the first turn.
Used cards go to the discard pile.
The team loses if all heroes on the same team lose all HP.
If the deck has no cards when drawing, take damage equal to the maximum HP.
