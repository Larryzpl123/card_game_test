States:
Most skills with state effects specify the number of layers, maximum layers, and duration.
Negative states:
Control type:
Slowdown/Slow/Sluggish
Cannot use action cards
Bound/Restricted
Cannot use action or equipment cards
Paralyzed
Cannot use action, equipment, or decision cards
Rooted
Cannot use hero active skills or action cards
Frozen/Ice Sealed
Cannot use hero active skills, action, or equipment cards
Suppressed
Cannot use hero active skills, action, equipment, or decision cards
Sealed
Cannot use hero active skills, equipment skills, action, equipment, or decision cards
Banished
Cannot use hero active skills, passive skills, equipment skills, action, equipment, or decision cards
Damage type:
Poisoned
Take 10 damage per turn, stackable
Bleeding
Take 10 damage + number of turns damage has been taken (first turn 10, second turn 20, and so on).
Positive states:
Inspired
Draw one extra card at the start of each turn, stackable
High Spirits
For attack [action] cards, rolls of 1, 3, or 6 all count as crits
Revenge
Each layer increases the next attack by 10 damage, clear all layers after attacking.
Special states
Damage type:
Terminal
Take 10 damage per turn, stackable, no layer limit, infinite duration, cannot be removed by any means
